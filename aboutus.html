    •  The National Institute of Technology, Durgapur (formerly Regional Engineering College, Durgapur),was established
    
   by an Act of Parliament in 1960. The Institute is a University which awards B.Tech., M.C.A., M.Sc., M.B.A.,M.Tech. and
    
   Ph.D. degrees to students after their successful completion of the specified courses. The Institute imparts education in
    
   the disciplines of Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, 
    
   Electronics and Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Metallurgical and Materials Engineering,Information
    
   Technology, Biotechnology, Chemistry, Mathematics, Environmental science, Materials Science and Management Studies. 
    
   As decided by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India, the procedure for selection of candidates for
    
   admission to the Bachelor Degree Courses in Engineering/Technology in NIT Durgapur and in other NITs
    
   is on the basis of State Rank/ All India Rank (AIR) of AIEEE conducted by Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi
    
   the same is executed through counselling by Central Counselling Board, AIEEE under guidance from MHRD, GOI as per schedule
    
   notified by CCB. In addition to the normal intake, a few seats are reserved for Foreign Students who are nominated by 
    
   the Ministry of External Affairs, Government of India, and the Indian Council for Cultural Relations, Government of India.
